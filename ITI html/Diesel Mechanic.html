diesel mechanic

ITI Diesel Mechanic Trade is a vocational training course offered by Industrial Training Institutes (ITIs) in India. The course provides students with practical skills in diesel engine repair and maintenance and equips them for the role of a diesel mechanic. The duration of the course is usually two years and covers various topics related to diesel technology. The ITI Diesel Mechanic Trade course is designed to provide students with theoretical and practical knowledge of diesel engine technology. The course covers topics such as engine construction, fuel injection systems, cooling systems, and electrical systems. The students are also trained in safety procedures, equipment maintenance, and troubleshooting diesel engine problems. The course curriculum includes both theoretical and practical aspects, which provide students with hands-on experience in diesel engine technology. The students are taught using modern technologies and tools, which enable them to learn the latest trends in the industry. The faculty is highly qualified and experienced, and the teaching methodologies are designed to enable students to learn at their own pace.
