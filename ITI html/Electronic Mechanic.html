Electronic Mechanic

ITI Electronic Mechanic Trade is a vocational training course offered by Industrial Training Institutes (ITIs) in India. The course provides students with skills in the field of electronic technology and equips them for the role of an electronic mechanic or technician. The duration of the course is usually two years and covers various topics related to electronic technology. The ITI Electronic Mechanic Trade course is designed to provide students with theoretical and practical knowledge of electronic technology. The course covers topics such as electronic devices and circuits, digital electronics, microprocessors, and communication systems. The students are also trained in safety procedures, electronic equipment maintenance, and installation of electronic equipment. The course curriculum includes both theoretical and practical aspects, which provide students with hands-on experience in electronic technology. The students are taught using modern technologies and tools, which enable them to learn the latest trends in the industry. The faculty is highly qualified and experienced, and the teaching methodologies are designed to enable students to learn at their own pace.
