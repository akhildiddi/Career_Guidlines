COPA

ITI COPA (Computer Operator and Programming Assistant) is a vocational training course offered by Industrial Training Institutes (ITIs) in India. The course aims to provide students with practical skills in computer operation and programming. It is a one or two-year course depending on the institute and covers various topics such as computer fundamentals, programming languages, database management, networking, web designing, and other related subjects. The ITI COPA trade is a skill-oriented course, and students are taught using modern technologies and tools. The course is designed to provide students with practical exposure to the latest trends in the industry. The teaching methodologies are designed to enable students to learn at their own pace, and the faculty is highly qualified and experienced.
