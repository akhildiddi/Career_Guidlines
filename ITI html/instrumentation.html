instrumentation

ITI Instrumentation Trade is a vocational training course offered by Industrial Training Institutes (ITIs) in India. The course provides students with practical skills in instrumentation technology and equips them for the role of an instrumentation technician or engineer. The duration of the course is usually two years and covers various topics related to instrumentation technology. The ITI Instrumentation Trade course is designed to provide students with theoretical and practical knowledge of instrumentation technology. The course covers topics such as measurement techniques, control systems, process control, and instrumentation systems. The students are also trained in safety procedures, calibration procedures, and installation of instrumentation equipment. The course curriculum includes both theoretical and practical aspects, which provide students with hands-on experience in instrumentation technology. The students are taught using modern technologies and tools, which enable them to learn the latest trends in the industry. The faculty is highly qualified and experienced, and the teaching methodologies are designed to enable students to learn at their own pace.
