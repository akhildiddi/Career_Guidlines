fitter

ITI Fitter Trade is a vocational training course offered by Industrial Training Institutes (ITIs) in India. The course provides students with practical skills in fitting and equips them for the role of a fitter or maintenance technician. The duration of the course is usually two years and covers various topics related to fitting technology. The ITI Fitter Trade course is designed to provide students with theoretical and practical knowledge of fitting technology. The course covers topics such as measuring tools, cutting tools, metal joining techniques, and machining processes. The students are also trained in safety procedures, equipment maintenance, and installation of mechanical components. The course curriculum includes both theoretical and practical aspects, which provide students with hands-on experience in fitting technology. The students are taught using modern technologies and tools, which enable them to learn the latest trends in the industry. The faculty is highly qualified and experienced, and the teaching methodologies are designed to enable students to learn at their own pace.
