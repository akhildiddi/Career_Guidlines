mechanic

Mechanic trade is a vocational training program offered by Industrial Training Institutes (ITIs) in India. The course aims to provide students with practical skills and theoretical knowledge to maintain, repair and troubleshoot machinery and equipment used in various industries. The course is designed to cover a broad range of topics related to mechanics, including mechanics of machines, vehicles, and other equipment. The course duration of mechanic trade in ITI varies from 1 year to 2 years, depending on the state and institute. The curriculum typically includes both theoretical and practical aspects to provide students with hands-on experience. The course syllabus may vary from institute to institute, but generally includes topics such as engine construction, transmission systems, brake systems, steering systems, suspension systems, and electrical systems. The Mechanic Trade course prepares students for a career as a mechanic, technician, or service engineer in various industries, including automotive, heavy machinery, aviation, and marine. Graduates can work in roles such as automobile mechanic, diesel mechanic, aircraft technician, heavy machinery technician, and marine engineer.
