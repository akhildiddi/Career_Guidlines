solar

ITI Solar Trade is a vocational training course offered by Industrial Training Institutes (ITIs) in India. The course is designed to provide students with practical skills in the installation, maintenance, and repair of solar panels and systems. With the increasing demand for clean energy, solar technology is becoming increasingly important, making the ITI Solar Trade course an excellent option for students who want to pursue a career in the renewable energy industry. The ITI Solar Trade course is a two-year program that covers various topics related to solar technology. The course curriculum includes theoretical and practical aspects, enabling students to learn about solar panels, solar inverters, batteries, and charge controllers. The students are also trained in safety procedures, equipment maintenance, and troubleshooting solar panel problems. The course content is designed to give students a comprehensive understanding of solar panel installation and maintenance. The students learn about the different types of solar panels, including monocrystalline, polycrystalline, and thin-film panels. They also learn about the different types of solar inverters and their functions, as well as the types of batteries used in solar systems.
