ITI Welder Trade is a vocational training course offered by Industrial Training Institutes (ITIs) in India. The course provides students with practical skills in welding and equips them for the role of a welder or welding technician. The duration of the course is usually one or two years and covers various topics related to welding technology. The ITI Welder Trade course is designed to provide students with theoretical and practical knowledge of welding technology. The course covers topics such as welding processes, welding techniques, welding safety, and welding equipment. The students are also trained in equipment maintenance and installation of welding systems. The ITI Welder Trade course also prepares students for higher education in welding technology and related fields. After completing the course, students can pursue diploma or degree courses in welding engineering or other related fields. The ITI Welder Trade course is a popular choice among students due to the high demand for skilled welders and welding technicians in the industry. The demand for welding services is increasing rapidly, and the need for skilled professionals who can install, maintain, and repair welding systems is also increasing.
